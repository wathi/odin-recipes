Lasagna

Description

"The béchamel adds a little something special to this wonderful lasagna. I like to use part skim ricotta and mozzarella and low-fat milk to cut some of the fat in this dish. You won't notice the difference," says recipe creator Valerie Brunmeier

Be sure to use a lasagna pan that's larger than 9x13 to prevent the casserole from bubbling over, she adds.

Ingredients

  • beef-and-pork combo
  • whole milk mozzarella and ricotta
  • béchamel
  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
